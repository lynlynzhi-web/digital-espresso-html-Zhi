Kape Pa More is a cozy neighborhood coffee shop serving freshly brewed local and international coffee blends,
handcrafted espresso drinks, and delicious comfort bites. With its warm ambiance, friendly baristas, and inviting
space to relax or catch up with friends, Kape Pa More is the perfect spot to sip, unwind, and enjoy “one cup more."
